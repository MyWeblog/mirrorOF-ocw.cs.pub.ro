Index

Here all the pages in the OSP namespace on OCW. There are three types of pages:

  • Public visible pages: These are pages that have publicly accessible links on the main page or on the sidebar.
  • Public non-visible pages: These are pages that may be read but have no accessible links on the main page or on the sidebar. You need explicit links to access them.
  • Private pages: These pages are only accessible to the OSP team.
